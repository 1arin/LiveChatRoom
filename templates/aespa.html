aespa

Aespa is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment. The group debuted in 2020 and consists of four members: Karina, Giselle, Winter, and Ningning. Aespa gained attention for their unique concept which integrates virtual avatars, known as "æ-members," into their performances and promotions. They have quickly risen to prominence in the K-pop scene, known for their energetic performances, strong vocals, and captivating stage presence.
